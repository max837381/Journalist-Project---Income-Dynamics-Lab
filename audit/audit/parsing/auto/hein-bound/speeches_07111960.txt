860272367|"Mr. President. I ask unanimous consent to have printed in the Appendix of the RECORD the speech of the Honorable Robert A. Lovett. former Secretary of Defense. at the George C. Marshall memorial dinner. August 10. during the annual meeting of the Association of the U.S. Army. Mr. Lovetts speech is a stirring document. one filled with warm tribute to a great soldierstatesman. General Marshall. and one containing sound advice on vital defense matters. I commend Mr. Lovetts speech to all of my colleagues and to the American people. Appropriately. the Association of the U.S. Army awarded to Mr. Lovett its first George Catlett Marshall medal ""for selfless service to the United States of America."" I know of no living American more deserving of this recognition."
